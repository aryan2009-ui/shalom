Who Should Not Do Surya Namaskar?
Pregnant women should not practice this after third month of pregnancy. Patients of Hernia and high blood pressure are warned against this practice. People suffering from back conditions should seek proper advice before commencing Surya Namaskar. Women should avoid Surya Namaskar during menses.
